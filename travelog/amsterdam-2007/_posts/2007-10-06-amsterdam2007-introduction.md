In November of 2006, I was scheduled to travel to France (again) for a friend’s birthday party. Unfortunately, I missed the flight….

The airline tickets were on Delta Airlines and they gave me credit for another flight of similar value to use within a year. There was some minimal cost for re-booking the flight to another destination so I basically had my choice of European destinations for another vacation.

I chose Amsterdam and traveled there in October, 2007. It was only a long weekend, but I did lots of walking around. I had no automobile (which is good) so I relied on public transportation. Public transportation and cycling in northern Europe is excellent – no auto is really needed.

The weather was mostly cloudy and a bit rainy, but I was in Amsterdam.

This travel log is mostly just pictures. I walked around Amsterdam for 3 days and visited a friend for a few hours on the last day before leaving.

Enjoy.
